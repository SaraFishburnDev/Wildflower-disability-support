Who We Are

Caring for
the Community

Wildflower DSS is a small, family-owned disability support provider serving NDIS participants across the Lockyer Valley, Ipswich & surrounds. We promote independence in everyday life with dignity and care.

Our team of qualified and experienced support workers are passionate about making a real difference. We take the time to understand your unique needs and create a support plan that works for you. Whether it's domestic assistance, getting out in the community, respite, or supported independent living — we're here for you.

Compassion First

Kindness is at the heart of everything we do.

Person-Centred

Your goals and preferences always come first.

Safe & Trusted

All staff are fully screened and trained.

Open communication with you and your family.

Our Services

How We Can Help You

We offer a range of NDIS-funded support services tailored to your individual goals across the Lockyer Valley, Ipswich & surrounds. Wildflower DSS is here to help you live your best life.

Domestic Assistance

Assistance with daily tasks like cooking, cleaning, personal care, and household duties — right in the comfort of your own home.

Enquire Now

Respite / STA

Short Term Accommodation and respite services that give family carers a well-deserved break, while your loved one receives outstanding support.

Enquire Now

Community Access

We help you stay connected — from social outings and shopping trips to attending appointments, events, and recreational activities.

Enquire Now

Yard Maintenance

Keeping your outdoor spaces safe, tidy, and enjoyable with regular lawn mowing, garden care, and general yard upkeep.

Enquire Now

Supported Independent Living (SIL)

24/7 support in a shared or individual living arrangement, helping you build daily living skills and live as independently as possible.

Enquire Now
